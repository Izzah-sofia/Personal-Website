🚶 WALKING 🚶

♣ I like to go for brisk walks as a way to relieve stress, to find an idea, or exercise. Walking while listening to music is a therapy for me, especially when I feel overwhelmed by assignment or just need some time alone.

♣ I became interested in this hobby after SPM. My mother often ask me to accompany her to brisk walk whenever she had free time or on weekends. We usually walk at night around Masjid Putra area or early in the morning at recreation park. My family also enjoy this activity too and we usually will walk between 3 km to 5 km.

♣ Since I start to go to UiTM, I usually go for brisk walk in the morning. Just to keep my mind in peace and protect my sanity.
