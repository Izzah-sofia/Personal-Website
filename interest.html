👐 SIGN LANGUAGE 👐

♣ I start to become interested in learning sign language after I join a program under UiTM in collaboration with an external organization that works with children with disabilities. The program was really interesting, as student can interact and do activities together with the children. The activities included traditional dance, badminton, batik painting, and more.

♣ I was actually inspired by this program to learn sign language, and I started to get excited about learning it during last semester break. I didn't know much about sign language because I had just learned the basics, and I needed to keep practicing so I wouldn't forget them easily. I started to forget a lot of basic things since the new semester started and I had assignments to complete. However, I tried to learn it whenever I had free time.

♪ J-POP GROUP: JO1 ♪

♣ JO1 is a global boy group formed by 11 people selected from Japan's largest audition program, Produce 101 Japan, which is a Japan version of Korea's audition program, Produce 101. This program aims to create a permanent boy group or girl group in music industry. Althought JO1 is a global boy group, all of them has Japanese nationality and come from Japan. Their songs are mostly in Japanese and their main market is more to Japan. However, JO1 also promotes the group and their songs in other countries such as Korea, America, Jakarta, and more.

♣ The reason I became fan of JO1 is that this group inspires me to keep working hard, even though I don't have experience in a certain field or when trying something new. Some of JO1 members had no background in music when they first joined the audition and participated in the program. However, they work hard to improve their singing and dancing skills until they could debut as JO1. Their hard work really inspired me to keep trying and learning new things, even though I don't have any experience to begin with.

His personality makes him one of the most loved members in &TEAM and among fans.
